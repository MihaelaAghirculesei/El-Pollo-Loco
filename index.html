The Legend of Pepe

Pepe, a courageous villager from a serene Mexican town, finds his peaceful home unexpectedly threatened by a flock of wild chickens. Renowned for his agility and bravery, Pepe boldly steps up to defend his village. With unwavering determination and a spark of heroism, he faces his feathery foes, striving to restore harmony and safeguard the place he cherishes.
